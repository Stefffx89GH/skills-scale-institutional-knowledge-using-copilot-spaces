# OctoAcme — Project Kickoff Checklist

## Purpose
Ensure all roles are aligned and accountable at the start of a project. Complete this checklist before or during the kickoff meeting.

**Related:** [Roles & Personas](./octoacme-roles-and-personas.md) · [RACI](./octoacme-role-interactions-and-raci.md) · [Project Initiation Guide](./octoacme-project-initiation.md)

---

## Pre-Kickoff Preparation

| # | Item | Owner | Done? |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Project One-pager drafted (problem, goal, success metrics) | Product Manager | [ ] |
| 2 | One-pager reviewed and approved by sponsor/stakeholders | Project Manager | [ ] |
| 3 | Core team identified and confirmed (PM, PdM, Tech Lead, Developers, QA, UX, BA) | Project Manager | [ ] |
| 4 | Kickoff meeting invite sent with agenda and pre-read docs | Project Manager | [ ] |
| 5 | High-level timeline and milestones proposed | Project Manager + Tech Lead | [ ] |
| 6 | Initial risk list drafted | Project Manager | [ ] |
| 7 | UX research or design dependencies identified | UX Designer | [ ] |
| 8 | Key business requirements or constraints documented | Business Analyst | [ ] |

---

## Kickoff Meeting Agenda Items

| # | Agenda Item | Facilitator | Done? |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Introductions — confirm team roles and responsibilities | Project Manager | [ ] |
| 2 | Present project One-pager: problem, goals, and success metrics | Product Manager | [ ] |
| 3 | Review high-level timeline and milestones | Project Manager | [ ] |
| 4 | Walk through initial architecture approach and constraints | Tech Lead / Architect | [ ] |
| 5 | Review UX approach and design deliverables timeline | UX Designer | [ ] |
| 6 | Clarify business requirements and stakeholder expectations | Business Analyst | [ ] |
| 7 | Discuss initial risks and dependencies | Project Manager | [ ] |
| 8 | Agree on QA approach and definition of "done" | QA Analyst | [ ] |
| 9 | Confirm communication cadence (standups, syncs, reporting) | Project Manager | [ ] |
| 10 | Confirm escalation paths and decision-making process | Project Manager | [ ] |

---

## Post-Kickoff Actions

| # | Item | Owner | Due |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Publish meeting notes and decisions | Project Manager | Within 24 hours |
| 2 | Create and share project board with initial backlog | Product Manager + BA | Within 2 days |
| 3 | Set up repo, environments, and CI skeleton | Tech Lead / Architect | Within 2 days |
| 4 | Begin detailed requirements documentation | Business Analyst | Within 1 week |
| 5 | Draft initial UX wireframes or user journeys | UX Designer | Within 1 week |
| 6 | Set up risk register with initial entries | Project Manager | Within 2 days |
| 7 | Confirm QA tools and test environment access | QA Analyst | Within 1 week |
| 8 | Share kickoff summary with Customer Support/Success | Project Manager | Within 2 days |

---

## Decision Gate
Move to full planning when:
- [ ] Team is aligned on goals, scope, and timeline
- [ ] Roles and owners are confirmed for key activities (see [RACI](./octoacme-role-interactions-and-raci.md))
- [ ] Initial risks are documented and owned
- [ ] Stakeholders have approved the One-pager
